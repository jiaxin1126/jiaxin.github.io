Circle of fifths

  • Reference: Wiki - Circle of fifths

Relationship between major and minor - Relative major and Relative Minor

  • eg: F# is the relative minor of A.
  • That means:
    • A major and F# minor share the same key signature.
    • Also share the same notes.
  • How about memorising the table above?
    • For me, who have enough performance exprience, memorising is the fastest way.

      • +1: Augmented

      • +1: Augmented
      • -1: Diminished
    • Minor:
      • +1: Major
