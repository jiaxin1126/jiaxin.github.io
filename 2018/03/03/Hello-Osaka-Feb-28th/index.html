Hello Osaka(Feb 28th)

Mar 3, 2018

Crowded Chuo-line and an expensive breakfast in Tokyo Station.
And then, very morning Shinkansen from Tokyo to Kyoto.

But when I arrived in Kyoto it was still not check-in time so…

Just 23 minutes by train, the second biggest city of Japan, a place I had been before 16 years ago!

OSAKA!

I’m not a good writer, you know.

Shin-Osaka station is for Shinkansen and Osaka station is for Osaka(?

Anyway, I had no plan so just got down on Osaka and got on a line which looks like Yamanote-line.
And then got down again at the station I knew the name.
