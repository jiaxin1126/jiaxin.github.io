Tones and Semitones

  • Major Scale
    • T T S T T T S
  • Natural Minor Scale
    • T S T T S T T
  • Harmonic Minor Scale
    • T S T T S (T+S) S
  • Melodic Minor Scale
    • T S T T T T S

Chords

Consider about a three-note chords(triad)

  • Perfect fifth
    • from the bottom to the top, 7 semitones, no matter what kind of scale
    • eg: C E G (T T S T), or A C E (T S T T)
  • Major third
    • from the bottom to the middle, 4 semitones
    • eg: C E (T T)
  • Minor third
    • from the bottom to the middle, 3 semitones
    • eg: A C (T S)
  • Three primary chords
    • C major, TONIC(中文：主音), built on the first note
    • F major, SUBDOMINANT(中文：下属音), built on the fourth note
    • G major, DOMINANT(中文：属音), built on the fifth note
